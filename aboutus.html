About Us

Welcome to Food Rescue, a platform dedicated to bridging the gap between excess food and those in need. Our mission is to reduce food waste by connecting food providers such as restaurants, households, and food industries with NGOs and charitable organizations. Through this initiative, we aim to contribute to a sustainable future while addressing hunger in our communities.

Founded in 2024, Food Rescue is powered by a passionate team committed to making a difference. We believe in creating a network where food is not wasted but instead finds its way to the tables of those who need it the most. Our platform enables users to easily register, donate, or request food with just a few clicks.

Join us in our mission to make the world a better place, one meal at a time. Together, we can fight hunger and reduce food waste, ensuring no food goes uneaten and no person goes hungry.
